Intensive Driving Courses in Surbiton

Surbiton is home — our office is on St Philip's Rd, and it's where most of our instructors start and end their working day.

Book Your Course Call 020 3143 1718

Kings Driving Centre is based on St Philip's Rd in Surbiton, a short walk from Surbiton station and the high street. If you're local, there's a good chance your instructor already knows your street.

Well connected, easy to reach

Surbiton's mainline station puts central London under 20 minutes away, which also means it's a genuinely easy base for us to run lessons across the wider area — Tolworth, Chessington, Kingston and Esher are all a short drive out.

A proper local team

Because we're physically based here, Surbiton learners tend to get the most flexible scheduling — early mornings before work, evenings, and weekend blocks are all easier to arrange when your instructor isn't driving across London to reach you.

Who this suits

Learners in Surbiton, Berrylands, and the surrounding streets who want an instructor who's genuinely local, not just "covers the area" from somewhere else.
