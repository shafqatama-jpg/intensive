Accessibility Statement

Last updated: 22 July 2026

We want this website, and our courses, to be usable by as many people as possible — that's true both of how we teach and how this site is built.

This website

We've built this site with semantic HTML, descriptive alt text on meaningful images, keyboard-navigable menus and forms, and colour contrast in mind throughout. We're aiming for WCAG 2.2 AA as a standard, and we treat this as ongoing work rather than a box we've ticked once.

Known limitations

Some embedded third-party content (our Google Reviews and YouTube widgets, provided by Elfsight) is outside our direct control, and its accessibility depends on that provider.

Our courses

We teach learners of all abilities and experience levels, including nervous drivers and learners with specific learning difficulties or disabilities — see Why Choose Us on the homepage. If you have a specific access requirement for your lessons, tell us when you enquire so we can match you with the right instructor and vehicle setup.

Reporting a problem

If you find part of this site hard to use, or need information in a different format, email info@kingsdrivingcentre.co.uk and we'll do our best to help.
