GDPR Policy

Last updated: 22 July 2026

This page summarises how Kings Driving Centre complies with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018. For the detail of what we collect and your rights, see our Privacy Policy.

Data controller

Kings Driving Centre, St Philip's Rd, Surbiton KT6 4DU, is the data controller for personal data collected through this website.

Lawful basis for processing

We process your enquiry and booking details on the basis of taking steps to enter a contract with you (arranging your course) and, for analytics and embedded review/video content, on the basis of your consent via the cookie banner.

Data minimisation

We only ask for the information we need to arrange and deliver your course.

International transfers

Some of our service providers (including Google and Formspree) may process data outside the UK/EEA. Where this happens, they do so under recognised safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses.

Your rights & complaints

You can ask to access, correct, or delete your data at any time via info@kingsdrivingcentre.co.uk. If you remain unhappy with how we've handled a request, you have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk.
