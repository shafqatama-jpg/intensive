Cookie Policy

Last updated: 22 July 2026

This page explains the cookies used on this website and how you can control them. When you first visit, you'll see a banner letting you accept all cookies or continue with necessary-only.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help websites function and, where you allow it, help us understand how the site is used.

Cookies we use

Strictly necessary

Used to remember your cookie preference itself and to keep the site functioning. These can't be switched off as the site wouldn't work properly without them.

Analytics (only with your consent)

We use Google Tag Manager to understand how visitors use the site, which pages are popular, and where to improve. This only loads after you click "Accept all" on the cookie banner.

Embedded content (only with your consent)

Our Google Reviews and YouTube video widgets are provided by Elfsight and only load once you've accepted cookies, as they set their own cookies to function.

Managing your preference

You can change your mind at any time by clearing your browser's local storage for this site, or by clearing cookies in your browser settings, which will show the banner again on your next visit.

More information

For general information about cookies, visit ico.org.uk.
