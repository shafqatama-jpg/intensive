HOW IT WORKS

Your route to passing

Every course follows the same route, compressed into as many consecutive days as you need — not spread thinly over months.

  • 01Day 1 — First lesson. We assess your level, ability and preferred gearbox, and set the plan.
  • 02Route & manoeuvre practice. Training on the actual Tolworth and Morden test routes.
  • 03Mock test. A full run-through under test conditions before the real thing.
  • 04Test day — pass! Use our dual-control car, manual or automatic, for the test itself.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How many hours of lessons will I need?

It depends on your experience — most learners with some prior driving practice do well with our 15 or 20 hour courses, while complete beginners often choose the 30 hour course. Your instructor can recommend a course length after an initial assessment.

Can I pay in instalments?

Yes — instalment plans are available through Klarna at checkout, alongside deposit and full-payment options.

Do you offer lessons in languages other than English?

Yes — our instructors can teach in English, German, Hindi, Urdu and Punjabi, so you can learn in whichever language you're most comfortable with.

Can I learn in an automatic instead of a manual car?

Yes — we teach in both manual and automatic cars, including for your test day itself, with a modern dual-control vehicle either way.

I'm a nervous learner or have struggled with driving before — can you help?

Yes — we teach learners of all abilities and experience levels, including nervous drivers, learners returning after a bad experience elsewhere, and anyone with a specific learning difficulty. Lessons are adapted to how you learn best.

I already have a test booked soon — can you still help?

Yes — our Urgent Test Cover service prioritises learners with a near-term test date at the Tolworth or Morden test centres and matches you with an instructor who knows the routes.

Which areas do you cover?

We teach across Tolworth, Surbiton, Chessington, Kingston upon Thames, Worcester Park, New Malden, Wimbledon, Esher, Epsom, Sutton and the surrounding area, within roughly a 6-mile radius.
