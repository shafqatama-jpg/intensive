Payment Policy

Last updated: 22 July 2026

This page explains how payment works when you book a course with us.

Accepted payment methods

We accept Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Apple Pay, Google Pay, PayPal, bank transfer, and instalment plans via Klarna.

How payment is arranged

Submitting the online booking form starts an enquiry, not an automatic charge — a member of our team will confirm your course and take payment directly, by card link, bank transfer, or in person, once details are confirmed.

Deposits, full payment, and instalments

You can pay in full, pay a deposit to secure your course dates with the balance due before the course starts, or spread the cost through Klarna. Klarna instalment plans are subject to Klarna's own eligibility checks and terms, separate from ours.

Currency and pricing

All prices on this site are shown in British Pounds (GBP) and include VAT where applicable.

Invoices

An invoice or payment receipt is provided for every course booked — just ask if you need one for your records.
